# Gear Inventory

**Domain:** outdoor / craft gear tracking

**Patron-style sentence:**

> Track my gear — what I own, where it is, what condition, when it was last checked.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Inventory tools fail when they treat items as static. Gear lives in cycles: acquired → used on trips → inspected → maintained → retired. The trip + packing-list pattern gives the patron a record of what they actually used, which is the question "should I keep this?" rests on. Location enum prevents the inventory from being a fiction.
