# Business Ledger

**Domain:** small-business bookkeeping

**Patron-style sentence:**

> I keep the books for a small business — transactions, accounts, customers, invoices, simple reports.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Bookkeeping is fundamentally double-entry: every value flows between accounts. Transaction is the central entity; account is what it moves between; customer attributes the income/expense. Category enables P&L. Reconciliation flag enables the "did this match the bank statement" check that defines real bookkeeping (not just spreadsheet tracking).
