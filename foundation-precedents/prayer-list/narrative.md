# Prayer List

**Domain:** personal prayer practice

**Patron-style sentence:**

> Keep a list of people and things to pray for — when I added them, when answered.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Prayer lists are deeply personal. The simplest schema (one entity, modest fields) respects that. Answered-at + answer-notes capture the gratitude practice that distinguishes a prayer list from a worry list. Category enables prayer-by-context (morning family, weekly community). The patron likely never asked for more entities; they would feel they were being managed.
