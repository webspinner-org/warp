# Photo Notes

**Domain:** photo organization and notes

**Patron-style sentence:**

> Catalog photos — when and where they were taken, who is in them, what the story is.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Family photo organization fails when the metadata is reduced to EXIF. The patron wants to capture _story_ (what was happening) and _people_ (who is in it, what was their relationship). The physical-vs-digital location split matters because most patrons inherit boxes of prints alongside their digital archive.
