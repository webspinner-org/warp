# Contacts

**Domain:** personal contact management

**Patron-style sentence:**

> Track people I know — how I met them, when we last talked, anything I want to remember.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Personal contacts apps fail by trying to be CRMs. The contact entity captures who they are; the interaction entity captures when you last talked. Met-how is the field that turns a name into a memory. Birthday is the field that turns an acquaintance into a friend. Do-not-contact is the field that respects boundaries.
