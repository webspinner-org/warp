# Livestock Records

**Domain:** small-farm animal records

**Patron-style sentence:**

> Track animals on the farm — births, breeding, vet, feed, sales.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Livestock records have legal and economic stakes. Tag is required because the regulatory requirement is per-animal. Sire/dam by tag (not by link) keeps lineage navigable even when the parents have left the herd. Breeding events are first-class — a small farm runs on them. Status enum tracks the realistic dispositions including non-sale exits.
