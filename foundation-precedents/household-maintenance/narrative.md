# Home Care

**Domain:** household maintenance

**Patron-style sentence:**

> Track stuff in the house that needs maintenance — filters, batteries, service appointments.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Home maintenance is about not-forgetting. Item + service-log lets the patron see when something was last touched. Next-due-at + replace-every-months together let the renderer surface what is overdue. The pattern beats calendar reminders because the schema lives with the thing being maintained, not with a date.
