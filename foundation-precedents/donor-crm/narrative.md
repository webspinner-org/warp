# Donor Records

**Domain:** nonprofit donor tracking

**Patron-style sentence:**

> I manage donors for a small nonprofit — gifts, contacts, campaigns, thank-you letters sent.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Nonprofit gift tracking needs donor stewardship signals built in (thank-you-sent, do-not-contact) — without them, the system is a glorified spreadsheet. Campaigns provide aggregation context that matters for grant reporting. Restricted flag matters for audit; method matters for reconciliation against the bank.
