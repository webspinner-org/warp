# Garden Log

**Domain:** garden tracking

**Patron-style sentence:**

> I want to track plants in my garden — what I planted, when, how I water and feed them, what I harvest.

**Why these entities, not others:**

Gardening is event-driven: plants are stable entities; waterings, feedings, harvests are events keyed to a plant. Separate event entities (vs one giant "log") let the patron sort and aggregate per kind. Location + sun on the plant capture the standing conditions that matter for diagnosing trouble.
